FAQ

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a marathon of innovation where teams and individuals come together to design and build something cool and compete for prizes.

Who can apply?

Anyone anywhere. The Hackathon is borderless and virtual.

Do I need a team?

No, you can enter alone, or team up with another developer via our Slack.

What can I build?

Whatever you like, so long as it uses Ethereum, and works inside of Status.
